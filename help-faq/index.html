    Product Care & Authenticity +

    Caring for Your Artisan Pieces

    Each piece is handcrafted by skilled artisans using traditional methods. To preserve their beauty:

    • Dust regularly with a soft, dry cloth
    • Keep away from direct sunlight and moisture
    • Handle with care - these are authentic, handmade items
    • For textiles, professional cleaning is recommended

    Authenticity Guarantee

    All our pieces are sourced directly from African artisans and collectives. Each item comes with information about its origin and the artisan community that created it.
